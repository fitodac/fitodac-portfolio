FAQ

Can I use one license in more than one computer?

No. Each Play license you get, will be limited to one computer only. So, if you are acquiring 3 licenses for your team. You are able to use Play on 3 computers.

For paid plans you can access Cloud from any computer as it works as an online video management platform. You´ll be able to upload/download videos from any computer.

50 minutes on Individuals Plan means I can only upload 50 minutes of video?

No. You are allowed to upload unlimited amount of videos in Cloud, but you will only be able to process 50 minutes for Automatic Detection.
